What is Antivirus Software?

Cybercrime is becoming more and more common, causing devastation financially, emotionally, and physically. To combat this, companies have been set up to help prevent these threats and protect you from cybercrime. The method to do so is known as Antivirus Software, which works to both prevent new cyber attacks and monitor for existing ones.

Why is Antivirus Software important?

The main purpose of antivirus software is to protect your devices and data from cyber threats. Different providers have different methods to do so, but many offerings typically include all or most of the following:

  • Protection against the types of Malicious Software (Malware).
  • Safeguarding and protection of data.
  • Improvement of System Performance.
  • Computer scanning for any threats.
  • Email and Web Protection against online threats.

Choosing the correct Antivirus

These days, there are so many choices for antivirus that it can sometimes be overwhelming to choose the "best" one. However, the below list are some things to consider when making your choice:

  • Be sure to do your research, and check the user reviews of the products.
  • Choose an antivirus product with an easy to use interface.
  • Be sure that real-time protection is offered, to avoid new threats.
  • Use comparison websites, to compare features.
