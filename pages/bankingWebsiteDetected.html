Before you proceed with making a payment, please note that Fraudsters can
pretend to be genuine companies, such as banks. They can even copy genuine
phone numbers and sound professional to trick you into thinking they are real.

Your bank will never ask for you to:

  • Provide your PIN number and/or passwords.
  • Request for you to transfer your money from one account to another.
  • Send you a Text or Email providing them with your Email Address.
